Fisherman's Quarter

The medieval district, built at the mouth of the river. Blau consists of traditional half-timbered houses, winding streets and numerous stone bridges.
Most of the buildings in the quarter are directly on the water, which is why Fishermen's Quarter is often compared to Italian Venice.
